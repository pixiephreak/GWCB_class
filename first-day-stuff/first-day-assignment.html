    How to Make a Sandwich for a Bear

    1. Collect any edible organic matter.
    2. Identify two largest items and place them side by side, with the largest surface face down.
    3. Place all other edible items on top of on of the larger items.
    4. Place the other largest item on top of the pile of edible items.
    5. Leave sandwich within smelling-distance of a bear.
